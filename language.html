Explore Our Engaging Language Courses

In addition to soft skills, Leaders Education Centre offers dynamic language courses that help students master English and Arabic with confidence. Through interactive lessons and real-life communication practice, learners of all levels improve fluency, expand vocabulary, and build strong speaking and writing skills all in a supportive and engaging environment.

Leaders English Developement (LED) for kids

Fluent Futures Begin with Leaders English

  • LED is a dynamic program that helps children develop clear, confident, and expressive English.
  • Through fun activities, role-plays, and speaking practice, kids improve communication, active listening, and emotional expression preparing them for success in school and life.

Arabic Champs for Kids

Build Arabic Confidence Through Fun and Speaking

Arabic Champs is an interactive spoken Arabic course that helps young learners speak and understand Arabic through real-life conversation, songs, and creative activities.

The program uses engaging scenes, useful vocabulary, and fun dialogue to build fluency, confidence, and cultural connection perfect for both expat and local children.
