Our Story

Leaders Training Centre (LTC) is a provider of professional development courses that is led by Mr. Shoaib Farooq – a global position holder. In a short span of time, LTC has emerged as a trusted and reliable business partner of individuals and corporates for their training and development needs in Qatar. We aspire to provide unparalleled training services and become a life-long learning partner for working professionals by employing industry expert faculty that uses a combination of practical industry knowledge combined with management skills. Our areas of focus are on higher education pathways, professional development programs and vocational industry-based training programs.
