Who We Are

Leaders Education Center in Doha, Qatar is a trusted place where children and teenagers come to learn, grow, and shine. We’re known for providing fun, supportive, and high-quality education that helps every child reach their full potential.

Our Story

Leaders Education Center (LEC) began with a simple belief that every child holds the potential to succeed when given the right environment, guidance, and support. Located in the heart of Doha, Qatar, we proudly serve students and teenagers. Our mission is to nurture young minds by combining strong academic support with life-enriching skills. Whether it’s mastering school subjects, building confidence in public speaking, or preparing for entrance exams, our programs are designed to empower students every step of the way. With experienced educators, compassionate teaching, and engaging methods, LEC has become a place where children grow not only in knowledge but in confidence, creativity, and character. This is more than education; this is where future leaders begin their journey.
