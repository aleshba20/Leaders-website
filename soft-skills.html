Explore Our Soft Skills Courses

Develop Confidence, Creativity, and Leadership

At Leaders Education Center in Doha, Qatar, we offer a diverse range of Soft Skills training programs tailored to empower learners of all ages. Our courses focus on developing essential life skills such as communication, creativity, emotional intelligence, and leadership. Whether your child aspires to be a confident speaker, an innovative thinker, or a budding entrepreneur, our engaging and hands-on programs provide the ideal foundation for personal and academic growth. Leaders Education Center – Qatar’s premier hub for personalized tutoring and holistic education.

Leaders Communication

Empower Your Voice, Inspire with Impact

  • Students transform their communication skills and boost their confidence. Dive into dynamic workshops, conquer stage fright, engage in exciting role-playing, and collaborate in fun team-building activities. Plus, gain inspiration from successful entrepreneurs and communication experts!
  • The program teaches verbal and non-verbal communication, active listening, persuasive speaking, and emotional intelligence essential for school, work, and life.

Marketing Mavericks

Ignite Creativity, Master Modern Marketing

Explore the exciting world of marketing. Discover your target audience, develop a unique brand identity, and master social media marketing. Create compelling advertising campaigns and analyse market trends to uncover business opportunities. Dive in and become a marketing maven!

Through real-world examples and fun projects, students will explore how to create marketing strategies, design ads, and understand audience behavior nurturing both creativity and business sense.

Money Masters

Financial Confidence for the Next Generation

  • Students will learn the essentials of business finance, including revenue, expenses, profit margins, and cash flow. Develop practical financial plans for your ventures through budgeting exercises. To mastering the art of financial forecasting to make informed business decisions.

Biz Whiz

Build Young Entrepreneurs with Big Ideas

Biz Wizards, Building Your Business Empire™ for a thrilling entrepreneurial adventure! Refine your business ideas and master operations. Hone your pitching skills and unveil your comprehensive business plan. Let’s turn your dreams into a business empire!

Graphic Design

Unleash Creativity Through Visual Storytelling

  • Graphic Design introduces students to the world of digital creativity, teaching the fundamentals of layout, color theory, typography, and image editing through beginner-friendly tools.
  • Students will learn to design posters, logos, social media graphics, and more empowering them to express ideas visually and develop practical design skills that are useful for school, projects, and future careers.

Fine Arts and Crafts

Inspire Creativity Through Art and Imagination

Fine Arts and Crafts is a hands-on program designed to introduce students to artistic expression through drawing, painting, sculpting, and mixed media.

Children will explore textures, colors, patterns, and techniques as they create their own artwork. This course enhances creativity, patience, and self-confidence in a relaxed and encouraging environment.
